Welcome to Med Cabinet

Thinking about using Medical Cannabis? Already approved for Medical Marijuana? New to Medical Use or Seasoned User?

This app is for you. It's an app targeting mostly new cannabis consumers (especially those medical marijuana users trying to get off of pharmaceuticals). The app focuses on providing strain information and recommendations for those using/wanting to use cannabis as a means to battle medical conditions and ailments. Med Cabinet is here to help patients find the right strains, dosing, intake method, intake schedule through education and personalized recommendations based on the provided history you gain through using the app!

Key Features

Recommendations

The app will provide recommendations based on medical ailment, strain use with review, and the history you gain from the app. If you are new to medical marijuana use the app can help you not feel so lost and help give you a guide and education to help you make better decisions for treatment.

Strain Library

Want to look around at different strain information. If you cannot find the recommended strain in your area and want to look at information to help find what is close, or even just want to look around at what is available and for what ailment look no further than our strain library.

A simple beginner questionnaire and past use history (if applicable), is the starting point for your personal history of strain use. Taking the recommendations and filling out more information will increase your history for you to peruse for your convience. You will even be allowed to make notes on your personal history to help you keep up with why you liked a strain, dosage intake, dosage method, etc in your own words so you can have different routes when you cannot find a new strain or when one ailment may be acting up more than another.
